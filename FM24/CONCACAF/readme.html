Football Manager rule files for CONCACAF v24.6

In this package, you will find rule files for CONCACAF Member Nations.

I did my best to use realistic formats for all the leagues that are included. The rule files contain:

  • real divisions and cups with real formats from 2023
  • teams from the 2023 season
  • all nations have transfer windows, registration periods, tv dates, match rules, prize money - when I was able to find this in my online research

Compatibility

  • FM Database 24.3.0 (only)

Installation Guide

Copy the .fmf files for the nations you want into the folder "<My Documents>\Sports Interactive\Football Manager 2024\editor data\". The folder can be different if you have changed it in the FM24 preferences. The leagues should then appear the next time you start a new game in FM24 and chose advanced setup.
Make sure you have the checkbox "add players to playable teams" checked. This is recommended, otherwise you may end up with a lot of greyed-out players.

Known Issues in FM24

Issue #1 - Continental Cup Schedule Problems In FM24, the Central American Cup uses a host nation from Caribbean Nations incorrectly. This causes teams to "travel" to the cup for the entire group stage (the whole month of August). Teams participating in this cup will not play any domestic games during that month. This can seriously clog up the domestic schedule.
My advice is to use my CONCACAF continental cups file where I fixed this, and then those teams will play domestic league on saturday and continental cup on wednesday like they should.
Issue #2 - Continental cup teams wrong for Panama In FM24, the Central American Cup picks teams from Panama from the 2nd tier instead of the 1st tier (Liga Panamena).
My advice is to use my CONCACAF continental cups file where I fixed this, and then the correct teams from Panama will enter.
Issue #3 - Continental cup teams from wrong year In FM24, the Central American Cup picks qualified teams in December. While all the domestic leagues already finish in May/June. This causes teams to participate in the Central American Cup 1 year later.
I advise to use my CONCACAF continental cup file for a better experience, the competition is set up in July and picks teams on that date when (most) competitions have finished.

Coefficients

Unfortunately, the coefficients cannot be viewed in the game. I was unable to activate that page, which is there for e.g. Europe. So, Because it is invisible it is totally intransparent what the rankings for the countries are.
The coefficienta are averages based on the number of teams in continental competitions. Basically, nations get points for each team from that country.:

  • 2p for each match win
  • 1p for each match draw (a game that goes to penalties is concidered a draw)
Things I added:
  • 4p for first place in Central American Group Stage
  • 2p for second place in Central American Group Stage
  • 2p for first place in Caribbean Cup Group Stage
  • 1p for second place in Caribbean Cup Group Stage
  • Caribbean Shield does not add to coefficients (I tried but it totally messed up the system)
